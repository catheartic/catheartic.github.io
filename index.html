Our mission

Catheartic Magazine's mission strives to uplift conversations about youth mental health. As mental health becomes an increasingly important topic for teenagers, it is crucial to foster communities that uplift conversations that support teens and their wellness. Catheartic Magazine's goal is to give teenagers of tomorrow a platform to write and create work that lets their perspectives shine.
